Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RemindBot

General

What is RemindBot?
RemindBot is an AI-powered reminder assistant that works directly in WhatsApp and Telegram. You can set reminders using natural language, voice notes, and even in Hindi or Hinglish. No app downloads or signups required!
How do I get started?
Simply click on the "Start on WhatsApp" or "Try on Telegram" button on our homepage. Send a message like "Remind me to call mom tomorrow at 5pm" and you're all set! The bot will confirm your reminder and notify you at the right time.
Do I need to download an app?
No! RemindBot works entirely within WhatsApp and Telegram. You just need to have one of these messaging apps installed, which you probably already do.
Is RemindBot free?
Yes! RemindBot is currently 100% free with unlimited reminders. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Features

Can I use voice messages to set reminders?
Absolutely! Just send a voice note saying something like "Remind me to buy groceries tomorrow at 6pm" and RemindBot will understand and create your reminder.
Does it support Hindi and Hinglish?
Yes! You can set reminders in English, Hindi, or Hinglish. For example: "Kal subah 7 baje exercise karne ki yaad dilana" or "Remind me kal 3 baje meeting hai" both work perfectly.
Can I set recurring reminders?
Yes! You can set daily, weekly, monthly, or custom recurring reminders. Just say "Remind me to take medicine every day at 9am" or "Meeting every Monday at 3pm" and RemindBot handles the rest.
How do I view my pending reminders?
Simply message the bot with "Show my reminders" or "List pending reminders" and you'll get a complete list of all your upcoming reminders.
Can I delete or edit a reminder?
Yes! You can message "Delete reminder about [topic]" or "Cancel my 3pm reminder" to remove reminders. To edit, simply delete the old one and create a new reminder.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?
Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We use WhatsApp-grade security and never share your personal information with third parties. Your reminders are private and only accessible to you.
Do you store my messages?
We only store the reminder information you explicitly create. We don't store your entire chat history, only the reminders you've set and their details (time, message, recurrence).
How long is my data kept?
Active reminders are kept until they're completed. Your reminder history is stored for 15 days, after which it's automatically deleted. You can also manually delete your data anytime.

Technical

What time zone does RemindBot use?
RemindBot is optimized for Indian Standard Time (IST). All reminders are set and delivered in IST unless you specify otherwise.
What if I don't specify a time?
If you don't specify a time, RemindBot will ask you for clarification. For example, if you say "Remind me tomorrow about the meeting," the bot will ask "What time tomorrow?"
Can I use RemindBot offline?
You need an internet connection to set reminders, but once set, you'll receive notifications even if you're temporarily offline (as long as your phone has connectivity when the reminder is due).
What happens if I miss a reminder notification?
The reminder message will stay in your chat history, so you can always see it when you check your messages. For recurring reminders, the next occurrence will still trigger as scheduled.

Troubleshooting

The bot isn't responding. What should I do?
First, check your internet connection. If that's fine, try sending a simple message like "Hi" to restart the conversation. If issues persist, contact us through the support channels on our homepage.
I didn't receive a reminder notification. Why?
Check if you have notifications enabled for WhatsApp/Telegram on your phone. Also verify that the reminder was set correctly by asking "Show my reminders." If the issue continues, please contact support.
The bot misunderstood my reminder. What now?
You can delete the incorrect reminder and create a new one. Try being more specific with dates and times. For example, instead of "tomorrow morning," say "tomorrow at 9am."

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly through WhatsApp or Telegram. We're here to help! You can also check out our detailed guide for more information.
